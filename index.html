About Us

A smile or a frown, an ‘I Love You’ or an ‘I Hate You’ - to many of us, the dichotomy in these expressions and phrases is clear. Yet, for some, the difference lies behind a thick veil that they struggle to remove; to them it is all a monotone shade of grey.

For any conversation, understanding what the other person feels is crucial; for the people that we aim to help, conversations aren’t possible. For them, having any social connection is next to impossible. Thus, the problem that we have identified, is essentially the deficit of emotional recognition in many millions of people globally, and through our invention, we aim to solve it in its core.

Our heads-up display-based emotion recognition system, designed for persons with social deficiencies, is:

  • Mechanised
  • Lightweight
  • Portable

Features

Facial emotion interpretation

Studies have shown that persons diagnosed with ASD have difficulty judging what a person is feeling based on their facial expressions. With our solution, our users will no longer face this problem.

Analysing language

Our powerful yet lightweight model will use tried-and-tested NLP techniques to determine the sentiment of a conversation.

Real-time output

Our solution will provide real-time output making it easier for our users to navigate through real-life conversations.

Bone-conduction tech

Visually-impaired persons will have no difficulty interpreting our output with this novel and inclusive approach to our product.

Heads-up-display

Our custom-engineered HUD will be placed on a strong and durable frame, allowing for instant use.

Cheap

Our customer-first approach motivates us to provide our solution at the lowest costs possible.
